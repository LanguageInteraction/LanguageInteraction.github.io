We are the Language Interaction Lab at the Siebel School of Computing and Data Science at UIUC.

We work at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Human-Computer Interaction. Our mission is to design human-centric AI systems that reduce inequalities in knowledge access by bridging the gap between specialized information and human understanding. We focuses on evaluating the ability of AI to communicate complex concepts to diverse audiences, while simultaneously building interactive tools that augment how people read, write, and listen in knowledge-intensive fields like medicine, law, and science.
